# Carbon Project Certification

<Callout type="info" title="Certification Importance">
  If your project is part of a carbon market it will need to align with a certification standard. These standards ensure your project meets rigorous social, environmental, and carbon integrity criteria.
</Callout>


## **Common standards include:**

* **Plan Vivo**: Focused on community-based natural resource management
* **Verra (VCS + CCB)**: Widely used for carbon crediting and sustainable development co-benefits
* **Gold Standard**: High-integrity climate and development certification
* **ART-TREES, ACR, ISO 14064, and others**: Depending on geography and carbon methodology

<Callout type="info" title="Why It Matters">
Certification influences which documents you’ll need, which indicators to track, and who verifies your project. It also helps align your activities with global goals and market expectations.
</Callout>
