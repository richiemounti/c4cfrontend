# **Uploading Site Boundaries**

## Mapping Your Project Sites

If you have shapefiles or boundary maps specific to individual sites, you can upload them during site setup.

## **You can upload:**
## Supported File Formats

<Step number="1" title="GIS File Formats">
* **Shapefiles** (.shp + .shx + .dbf zipped together)
* **GeoJSON or KML files**
</Step>

<Step number="2" title="Alternative Formats">
* **Image maps or PDFs** with clear markings (if no GIS data)
</Step>


<Callout type="tip" title="Tips">
* Label site name, boundaries, and nearby landmarks
* Use local names and include a legend if possible
* Don't worry if it's not perfect — just be consistent
</Callout>

## **Why Site Mapping Matters**

<Callout type="info" title="Why It Matters">
Site-level mapping helps visualise where interventions take place and allows analysis by zone, ecology, or population.
</Callout>
