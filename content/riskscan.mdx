# **Project Risk Scanning**

## **Your scan should include anything that might disrupt or delay the project. For example:**
<Step number="1" title="Legal Risks">
* Land disputes, lack of clear permissions, pending court cases
</Step>

<Step number="2" title="Political Risks">
* Changes in leadership, community opposition, election periods
</Step>

<Step number="3" title="Geographic Access Risks">
* Flooding, remote areas, poor road infrastructure
</Step>

<Step number="4" title="Social Risks">
* Past failed projects, lack of trust, unresolved grievances
</Step>


## Why Risk Scanning Matters

<Callout type="info" title="Why It Matters">
Early risk identification allows for better mitigation and stakeholder planning. It also signals project readiness and integrity.
</Callout>
